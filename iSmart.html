What is iSmart?

iSmart is a state-of-the-art humanoid robot, powered by artificial intelligence, designed to bring interactive, engaging, and personalized teaching to every classroom.

With iSmart, educators can deliver customized lessons, manage attendance, and interact with students like never before, thanks to its hand gestures and head movements for a more natural experience.
