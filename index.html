Who We Are

We are a highly passionate team of educationists, technologists, and professionals with a combined industry experience of more than 200 man years. We have created iSpark Learning Solutions to bridge the huge gap in both digital divide and economic divide in education in India by fully leveraging education technologies. Using both online and blended teaching, we deliver meaningful education to all school students and develop their 21st-century skills that include innovation, creativity, problem-solving, critical thinking, computational thinking, logical reasoning, collaboration, communication, and so on. These skills are mandatory in order to get jobs and survive in this knowledge-based economy.

Learn More

Our Mission

To design, develop, and deliver the 21st-century skills-focused educational programs to school students and transform them holistically to be successful in this rapidly advancing knowledge economy. To indigenously design and develop courses and products to skill 300+ million school-going children in India and prepare them for the brave new world that values skills over marks. To design and develop our own courses focused on skills development aligned with NEP2020 and various kits for Robotics, AI, IoT, Electronics, etc., PC/Laptop, student engagement devices, etc., that are truly affordable to all students and bridge the gap in economic and digital divide in the country. To deliver high-quality and meaningful education online in a cost-effective way and reach out to all students wherever they are using our world-class interactive tele-education platform. We strive to create a National Knowledge Grid as dreamed by former president and great scientist Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam through our network of virtual learning cum innovation centres spanning India to deliver industry-relevant (Education 4.0) and meaningful education to all. To be the leading one-stop edtech organization in the world to offer both courses and educational kits that are skills-centric and very affordable.

Our Vision

To be the leading force in transforming education by designing, developing, and delivering innovative, skills-focused programs that empower over 300 million school students in India to succeed in the 21st-century knowledge economy. Our vision is to create a future where education is universally accessible, skill-centric, and aligned with the needs of a rapidly evolving global landscape. We aspire to design and develop our own courses and products that adhere to NEP2020 guidelines, offering Robotics, AI, IoT, and Electronics kits, along with student engagement devices like PCs and laptops, that are affordable for all and help bridge the economic and digital divide. Our goal is to deliver high-quality, impactful education online, reaching students wherever they are through our cutting-edge interactive tele-education platform. We are committed to realizing Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam's dream of a National Knowledge Grid by establishing a network of virtual learning and innovation centers across India, offering industry-relevant Education 4.0 that is both accessible and meaningful. We envision being the world's foremost edtech organization, providing a comprehensive range of courses and educational kits that are not only skills-centric but also affordable, ensuring that no student is left behind in this brave new world that values skills over marks.
