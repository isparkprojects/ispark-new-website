About iSpark Innovators

Transforming education through innovation and technology, empowering students with future skills.

Who We Are

iSpark's parent company, TCM Ltd, has a distinguished history spanning 91 years, founded by Dr. Sir CV Raman. We focus on nurturing 21st-century skills like Robotics, AI, IoT, Big Data, critical thinking, and creativity.

We Are Different

Our mission is aligned with NEP2020 to provide project-based experiential learning. Our curriculum and products focus on bridging the digital divide and developing essential skills.

Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize education by equipping students with essential 21st-century skills, preparing them to thrive in the AI and digital era.

Vision

Our vision is to be the global leader in providing accessible, affordable education, enabling students to succeed as global citizens.
